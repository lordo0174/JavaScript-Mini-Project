1. Which part did you find most difficult to implement?

The most challenging part was implementing the Game Over logic so that, upon losing, a screen appears displaying the total points achieved. It required precise timing to stop the game state while simultaneously capturing the final score to show it to the user.

2. Did you add any extra functionality (Bonus)?

In addition to the suggested bonuses, I modified the character's jump function. Instead of a 'linear' movement, I developed an aesthetic jump with a smoother arc, making the animation feel more fluid and less robotic.

3. Does it work correctly in all browsers?

I have specifically tested and confirmed the project's functionality in Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge, where the CSS Grid and animations render correctly.
